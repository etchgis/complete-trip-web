  • Sign Up
    • If you don’t already have an account, click Sign Up.
    • Enter your information, agree to the terms and conditions, and click Create to make an account.
    • Enter the 6-digit code that was sent to your email and click Submit.
      • Resend the code if you did not receive the email.
    • Enter your credentials and click Log In to access your new account.
    • Add your contact information as prompted and click Next.
    • Enter your home address and click Next.
      • If you skip this step, you will have to enter your home address when planning trips.
    • There is an ability to enter a primary coordinator for them to be able to track your trips. If that is wanted, enter their information and click Next. You may also choose to Skip that option.
    • Accessibility options can be specified. Click what is applicable and click Next.
      • If you skip this step, they can be changed later in settings.
    • Enter the 6-digit code that was sent via email.
  • Log In
    • If you have already created an account, click Log In.
    • Enter your credentials and click Log In.
    • Verify your identity by selecting a method of receiving a code.
    • Enter the 6-digit code that was sent and click Submit.
  • NFTA Community Shuttle
    • The NFTA Community Shuttle is an on-demand shuttle service operated by the NFTA. It takes you within the Buffalo-Niagara Medical Campus vicinity.
    • Click the NTFA Community Shuttle button.
    • Inside the green border are the options for travel. As you move around on the screen, the location will move with it.
    • Click Summon Shuttle when you have selected your pick-up location on the map.
    • Enter a location where you will be dropped off, the number of people riding, and click Confirm.
    • Your Your shuttle trip information will be displayed.
    • If you have questions or need assistance regarding your shuttle trip, you may call NFTA by pushing the button, which will display the number on the bottom of the screen.
    • Cancel the trip if you decide you no longer need shuttle services.
    • If you push the exit button, you will be brought to the main screen.
  • Upcoming Trips
    • To view the booked trip or any other upcoming trips, you can click the icon on the bottom left of the screen.
    • If you click See trip details, it will show you specifics.
    • You may favorite a trip by clicking on the star, naming the trip, and selecting Save.
    • Clicking Go shows turn-by-turn navigation.
  • Trip History
    • Clicking Trip History will take you to your trip log.
    • Under the Activity section, all trips you have taken will be listed.
    • Trips that have been favorited will be shown in the Favorites section.
  • Find Nearby Routes
    • This option allows you to explore quickly without scheduling anything.
    • Start by panning to a location on the map.
    • All routes in the vicinity will be shown and will update if panned to a different location on the map. They will be listed on the bottom.
    • If you click a route, all the stops will show on the map.
    • The map will pan to a stop if you click on one from the list.
  • Schedule New Trip
    • Click the Schedule New Trip button to book transportation.
    • Enter the locations, date, and time and click Next.
      • Travel times can be booked as:
        • Leave Now
        • Leave At
        • Arrive By
    • A location can be saved and assigned a nickname for future use.
    • Select the mode(s) of transportation you want for travel and click Submit.
    • Routes will be generated and show a list of options. Some walking with use of the bus may be required.
      • Some walking with use of the bus may be required.
    • You can select a route to preview it before scheduling.
    • The details of the trip will be displayed in a list form as well as shown on the map. Click Schedule Trip or go back if you’d like to review other options.
  • Schedule New Trip – Assistant
    • In the Schedule a Trip area, you have an option to use speech recognition to book trips.
    • Click the blue speech recognition tool to utilize that service.
    • Allow Complete Trip to access both speech recognition and the microphone.
    • Push the speech recognition button to begin speaking and interacting with the bot.
  • Profile and Settings
    • Profile Information
      • Click Profile Information and it will display your information.
      • Click Edit and Save for changes to your personal information.
      • Your account can be deleted by clicking Delete Account, typing the prompt, and clicking Delete Account once more.
    • Coordinators
      • This page will show coordinators associated with the account.
      • Pending means that the person has not accepted this request yet via email.
      • To remove a coordinator, click the trash can icon and confirm your request by selecting Delete.
      • Additional coordinators can be invited by clicking Invite a Coordinator, entering the necessary information, and selecting Invite.
    • Favorites
      • This is where favorited trips and locations will appear.
      • Clicking the button will remove it from the list.
    • NFTA Paratransit Access Line (PAL) Direct Users
      • o This will provide a direct link to schedule a PAL trip for users who are eligible.
    • Terms and Conditions
      • The terms and conditions of usage will be listed on this page.
    • Help
      • You will be directed to this document if you need any assistance.
    • Trip Preferences
      • This is where you can specify if you have special accommodations that need to be accounted for when booking a trip, such as wheelchair accessibility, the need for minimal walking, and/or having a service animal.
      • The number of transfers per trip can be modified, as well as the preferred mode(s) of transport.
    • Accessibility
      • The option for verbal directions can be turned on or off, as well as specifying the preferred display language.
    • Notifications
      • This is where you can customize preferences about trip notifications.
    • Password
      • Your password may be changed to something new on this page by entering the prompted information and clicking Submit.
